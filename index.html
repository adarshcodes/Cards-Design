Who We Are

We are a group of consultants-turned-teachers dedicated to teaching astrology. Our unique one-weekend-per-course system will enable you to learn so much within a short span of time. You will definitely surprise yourself! If you want to learn astrology simply for personal developement, or if you want to become a professional astrology consultant, you are in the right place.

What we do

We train hundreads of astrology students world wide and our student community has only been growing by the day. In addition to multiple levels of training, we are also proud providers of our own astrology software used for horoscope calculations and interpretations. Besides this, we also provide short-term post-training support and periodic webinars to help you keep in touch.

How we do it

Our methods are always scientific and standardized. Whilemost astrologers and clients view astrology as a mere sentiment, we have a totally down-to-earth view on the whole idea. We teach our students based solely on uniform calculations and consistent interpretations. We promise you that your viewpoint on astrology will definitely change after you attend our first class.
